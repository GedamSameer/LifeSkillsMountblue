# Focus Management

## Question 1

**What is Deep Work?**

**Answer:**

Professional activities performed in a state of distraction-free concentration that pushes cognitive abilities to the limit. These efforts create new values, improves for skills and are hard to replicate. - Cal Newport

## Question 2

**According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?**

**Answer:**

Neuroscientists have found that intense periods of focus in isolated fields of work cause myelin to develop in relevant areas of the brain. 
Deep work strategies 
1. Schedule your distraction periods
2. Develop a rhythmic deep work ritual 
3. Daily shutdown complete ritual 

## Question 3

**How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?**

**Answer:**

I am waking up early to complete a high cognitive task. I wake up early, put water on my face and start to study. After an hour or so, I take a break and do things like brushing my teeth, etc. Then again, get back to work. These morning hours when I have completed my sleep and just got up energized is the most effective time of my day. Any challenging task can be completed in this period of time. I have mastered deepwork and can do deep work till I have lunch from the time I wake up. 

## Question 4

**What are the dangers of social media, in brief?**

**Answer:**

Dangers of social media
1. Social media kills the attention span and your ability to deep work.
2. It's addictive
3. It kills time; you should actually rest, but you end up scrolling social media instead of resting. This reduces your rest time and disturbs your rest completely.
