# Grit and Growth Mindset

## Question 1

**Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few(1 or 2) lines. Use your own words.**

**Answer:**
The key trait of people who achieve success is grit. Developing a growth
mindset helps build that grit and allows individuals to improve through
effort and persistence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 2

**Paraphrased Summary:**

**Answer:**
There are two types of mindsets: fixed and growth. People with a growth
mindset believe skills can be developed through learning and effort, and
they focus on improving through practice.

**Four Key Ingredients of Growth:** 
1. Effort
2. Challenges
3. Mistakes
4. Feedback

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 3

**What is Internal Locus of Control?**
Internal locus of control means believing that you have control over
your own life and outcomes.

**Key Point from the Video:**
Develop the belief that your actions and decisions shape your results.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Question 4

**Key Points to Build a Growth Mindset:** 
- Believe in your ability to figure things out
- Maintain an open mindset
- Stay resilient after setbacks
- Honor and embrace the struggle
