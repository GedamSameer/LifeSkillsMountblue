## Question 3

**What are active and diffused modes of thinking?**

**Answer:**  
There are two modes of learning: focused (active) mode and diffused mode. In focused mode, you concentrate deeply on a task. In diffused mode, you are relaxed and allow your brain to make broader connections. Switching between these two modes helps you understand difficult concepts faster and more effectively.
